About Me

Hi, I'm Cate Loughlean, a mother of three, professional gift wrapper with over 30 years of experience. As a proud, long-standing member of the London community and the owner of Wrapped. I’m here to make your holiday gift-wrapping stress-free and enjoyable.

Curious how it works? If you live in London and need help wrapping your gifts, simply fill out the form. I’ll personally come to pick up your gifts at a designated time slot. Once they’re beautifully wrapped, I’ll return them to you, and you can pay for the service upon delivery. Both CASH & E-TRANSFER are accepted!

Let me take the hassle out of your holiday preparations. Fill out the form today to schedule a pickup and get an estimated completion date. Let’s make your holiday season a little brighter, one wrapped gift at a time!
